<p><strong>The Sulfur Selective Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfur Chemiluminescence Detector（SCD）</li><li>Flame Photometric Detector (FPD)</li><li>Mass Spectrometry Detector (MSD)</li><li>Atomic Emission Detection (AED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulfur selective detector market consists of various detector types, each serving unique analytical needs. The Sulfur Chemiluminescence Detector (SCD) excels in ultra-sensitive sulfur analysis through luminescent reactions. Flame Photometric Detectors (FPD) utilize the characteristic light emitted by sulfur in a flame for detection. Mass Spectrometry Detectors (MSD) offer qualitative and quantitative analysis through molecular fragmentation. Atomic Emission Detection (AED) identifies sulfur based on emitted light at specific wavelengths, enhancing elemental analysis capabilities. Together, they cater to diverse industrial and research applications.</p></p>
