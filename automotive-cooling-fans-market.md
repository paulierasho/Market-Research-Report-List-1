<p><p>The automotive cooling fans market consists primarily of electric and mechanical fans. Electric fans are commonly used in modern vehicles, offering efficient cooling and adjustable speed, improving fuel economy and reducing engine load. They activate based on temperature sensors, providing optimal airflow. In contrast, mechanical fans are driven by the engine's belts, operating at a constant speed. While they are simpler and often more affordable, they can be less efficient, especially at lower engine temperatures. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal engine performance.</p></p>
