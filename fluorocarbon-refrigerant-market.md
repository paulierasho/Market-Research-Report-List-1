<p><p>The fluorocarbon refrigerant market is categorized into three main types: Hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs), which are transitional substances with lower ozone depletion potential but still harmful; Hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs), used widely for their effective cooling properties while having a higher global warming potential; and Hydrofluoroolefins (HFOs), the newest option with minimal environmental impact and low global warming potential. HFOs represent a shift toward more sustainable refrigerants, aligning with global efforts to reduce climate change effects.</p></p>
