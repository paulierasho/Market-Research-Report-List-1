<p><p>The Automotive & Transportation Coatings market encompasses various types of coatings tailored for vehicle protection and aesthetics. Waterborne coatings are eco-friendly and offer reduced volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-borne coatings provide excellent durability and finish but have higher VOCs. Powder coatings are solvent-free and environmentally friendly, ideal for a hard, long-lasting finish. UV-cured coatings utilize ultraviolet light for rapid curing, enabling efficient production processes while delivering a strong, durable surface. Each type meets specific industry requirements and environmental standards.</p></p>
