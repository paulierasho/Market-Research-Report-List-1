<p><strong>The Polymer Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Stabilizers</li><li>Processing Stabilizers</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Sulfur-containing Co-stabilizers,</li><li>Metal Deactivators</li><li>Antistatic Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymer additives market encompasses various products that enhance the performance and longevity of polymers. Light stabilizers protect against UV degradation, while processing stabilizers improve processing efficiency and prevent degradation during manufacturing. Antioxidants inhibit oxidation, maintaining polymer integrity. Sulfur-containing co-stabilizers aid in stabilizing formulations, whereas metal deactivators neutralize metal catalysts that could lead to deterioration. Finally, antistatic agents reduce static electricity buildup, enhancing product usability and safety in applications. Together, these additives ensure improved durability and functionality of polymer materials.</p></p>
