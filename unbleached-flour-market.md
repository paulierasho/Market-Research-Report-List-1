<p><p>Unbleached flour is a type of wheat flour that has not undergone the chemical bleaching process that many flours do. Instead, it retains its natural color, which is slightly off-white or cream. The natural aging process allows unbleached flour to develop stronger gluten, making it ideal for various baked goods, including bread, pastries, and cookies.</p><p>The unbleached flour market is witnessing significant growth fueled by rising health consciousness and increasing demand for organic and natural food products.
