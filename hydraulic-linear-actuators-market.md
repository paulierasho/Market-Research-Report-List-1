<p><strong>The Hydraulic Linear Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic linear actuators market is primarily categorized into single-acting and double-acting actuators. Single-acting actuators utilize hydraulic pressure to move the piston in one direction, relying on a spring or external force for return motion, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, double-acting actuators allow hydraulic fluid to move the piston in both directions, providing greater control and force for applications requiring precise positioning and movement, thereby enhancing their utility in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
