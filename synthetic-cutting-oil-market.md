<p><p>The synthetic cutting oil market is primarily divided into two types: water-based cutting oil and oil-based cutting oil. Water-based cutting oils are formulated with emulsifiers and additives, providing excellent cooling properties and reduced environmental impact, making them ideal for a variety of machining processes. In contrast, oil-based cutting oils utilize mineral or synthetic oils, offering superior lubrication and protection against wear, often preferred for heavy-duty applications where extreme conditions are encountered, ensuring longer tool life and improved performance.</p></p>
