<p><p>The pharmaceutical filtration centrifuge market includes several types designed for specific processing needs. Horizontal peeler centrifuges efficiently separate solids from liquids, facilitating easy cake removal. Top discharge centrifuges allow for straightforward discharge of materials from the top, enhancing operational efficiency. Vertical peeler centrifuges feature a vertical design that aids in effective cake washing and drying. Inverting filter centrifuges provide a unique function of inverting the filter element for optimal solid-liquid separation, suitable for various pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
