<p><p>The handheld laser cleaning machine market encompasses two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse laser cleaning machines. CW handheld lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for maintaining consistent cleaning power for tasks like rust removal or surface preparation. In contrast, pulse handheld laser cleaning machines release bursts of energy, providing precise cleaning for delicate surfaces or intricate designs without damaging the substrate. Both types are increasingly utilized in various industries for efficient and eco-friendly cleaning solutions.</p></p>
