<p><strong>The SMD Thermistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC Type</li><li>PTC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMD thermistor market is primarily categorized into two types: NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) thermistors. NTC thermistors decrease in resistance as temperature rises, making them ideal for temperature sensing and compensation applications. Conversely, PTC thermistors increase resistance with rising temperature, often used in overcurrent protection and as resettable fuses. Both types serve critical roles in electronics, automotive, and consumer goods, contributing to enhanced performance, safety, and efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
