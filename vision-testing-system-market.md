<p><strong>The Vision Testing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ffERG (Full Field Electroretinography)</li><li>mfERG (Multifocal Electroretinography)</li><li>ERG (Pattern Electroretinography)</li><li>VEP (Visual Evoked Potential)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vision testing system market encompasses various technologies used to assess visual function. Full Field Electroretinography (ffERG) evaluates the overall retinal response to light. Multifocal Electroretinography (mfERG) assesses localized retinal function across different areas. Pattern Electroretinography (ERG) measures retinal responses to visual patterns, highlighting macular function. Visual Evoked Potential (VEP) analyzes brain responses to visual stimuli, providing insights into visual pathway integrity. Additionally, the "Other" category includes emerging technologies and specialized tests used in specific clinical applications.</p></p>
