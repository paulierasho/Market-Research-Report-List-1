<p><p>The electro-diagnostic equipment market comprises various technologies used to assess electrical activity in the body. Electromyogram (EMG) measures muscle response to electrical stimulation, while Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS) evaluate the speed and efficiency of nerve signal transmission. Electrocardiography (ECG) records heart electrical activity, and Electroencephalography (EEG) monitors brain wave patterns. Electroretinography (ERG) assesses retinal function, and Electrogastrography (EGG) tracks stomach electrical activity. These modalities are essential for diagnosing neurological, muscular, cardiac, and gastrointestinal conditions.</p></p>
