<p><strong>The Metric Roller Chains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Strand Chain</li><li>Multistrand Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metric roller chains market is categorized primarily into single strand chains and multistrand chains. Single strand chains are designed for simple applications and provide a straightforward linkage between sprockets, ideal for lighter loads. In contrast, multistrand chains consist of multiple connected roller chains, allowing for increased strength and load capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. This distinction helps industries select appropriate chains based on specific operational requirements, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
