<p><p>The pneumatic drives market comprises various types, including piston drives, rodless drives, swing drives, and others. Piston drives utilize compressed air to create linear motion, making them ideal for heavy lifting tasks. Rodless drives offer a compact design with a cylinder that allows for smooth, continuous motion without external components. Swing drives provide rotational movement typically used in material handling. Other pneumatic drive types include diaphragm and rotary drives, each designed for specific applications, enhancing efficiency and versatility in industrial processes.</p></p>
