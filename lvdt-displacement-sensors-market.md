<p><p>LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) displacement sensors are essential in precision measurement applications. The market is segmented into contact and non-contact types. Contact LVDTs operate by physically interacting with the object being measured, providing high accuracy and resolution, but may wear over time. Non-contact LVDTs utilize electromagnetic fields to detect displacement without physical contact, offering longer lifespans and minimizing wear and tear. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
