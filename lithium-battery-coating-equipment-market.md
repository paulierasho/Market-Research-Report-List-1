<p><ul><li>Extrusion Type Coater</li><li>Transfer Type Coater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lithium Battery Coating Equipment market includes Extrusion Type Coaters and Transfer Type Coaters. Extrusion Type Coaters utilize a continuous process to apply coatings directly onto battery components, ensuring uniform thickness and efficient production. In contrast, Transfer Type Coaters operate by applying coatings through a separate transfer mechanism, allowing for more precise control over the coating application and better adaptability to various substrate materials. Both types are essential for enhancing battery performance and longevity in lithium-ion technology.</p></p>
