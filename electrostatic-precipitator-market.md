<p><p>The electrostatic precipitator market is segmented into two main types: wet and dry. Wet electrostatic precipitators utilize a liquid medium to enhance particulate collection efficiency, making them effective for flue gas treatment containing sticky or hydrated particles. In contrast, dry electrostatic precipitators operate in a dry environment, using electrical charges to separate dust and pollutants from gases, often used in industries like coal-fired power plants. Both types serve critical roles in air pollution control and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
