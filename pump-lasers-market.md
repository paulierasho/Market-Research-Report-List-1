<p><p>The pump lasers market is primarily divided into two types: single mode and multi-mode pump lasers. Single mode pump lasers feature a narrow beam and are designed for applications requiring precise control and high efficiency, typically used in telecommunications and fiber optics. In contrast, multi-mode pump lasers have a broader beam and are utilized for applications necessitating higher power output, such as industrial laser cutting and medical devices. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing laser performance across various sectors.</p></p>
