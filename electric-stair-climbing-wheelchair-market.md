<p><p>The electric stair climbing wheelchair market is primarily segmented into two types: wheeled and orbital. Wheeled wheelchairs use traditional wheel designs, allowing users to navigate stairs by lifting the front wheels while the rear wheels support the transition. In contrast, orbital stair climbers feature specialized rotating wheels that facilitate smooth movement up and down stairs, providing enhanced stability and ease of use. Both types cater to varying user needs, enhancing mobility and accessibility for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
