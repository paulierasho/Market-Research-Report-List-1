<p><p>A Safety Instrumented System (SIS) is essential for ensuring safety in industrial processes, mitigating risks associated with hazardous events. The market is segmented into three key types: </p><p>1. **Sensors** detect unsafe conditions, providing critical data for intervention.</p><p>2. **Logic Solvers** process sensor inputs and determine whether safety actions should be initiated.</p><p>3. **Final Elements** are actuators or mechanisms that execute safety measures, such as shutting down equipment. Together, these components maintain operational safety and regulatory compliance across various industries.</p></p>
