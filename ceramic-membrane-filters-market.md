<p><p>Ceramic membrane filters are advanced filtration solutions used in various industries for their durability and efficiency. The market is primarily divided into two types: flat-sheet and pipe membrane filters. Flat-sheet membrane filters are designed for clarification and microfiltration, offering a large surface area for effective filtration. In contrast, pipe membrane filters are used in tubular or cylindrical formats, allowing for higher flow rates and better handling of viscous fluids. Both types provide excellent chemical resistance and longevity in challenging applications.</p></p>
